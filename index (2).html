The Shadow Phase is the first and most rewarding presale phase. Buyers receive a +30% token bonus on top of the base rate of ~1,875,000 $KOKO per 1 SOL. Minimum purchase is 0.2 SOL and maximum is 5 SOL per wallet. Once the Shadow Phase closes, the bonus drops to +20% in the Emergence Phase.
